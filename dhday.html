My contribution

At the outset, it was not self-evident that this project would yield a new application. My early research and problem-framing was crucial for helping product management and technical leadership make the decision to build a new application, Student Performance Record. Business process analysis helped demonstrate the pros and cons of creating new capabilities in existing applications versus building a new application solely for providing records access. Later, I created strategic design assets that allowed us to readily talk about our approach to the project in terms that business stakeholders could relate to. Finally, I developed the UI alongside front-end development team members; this collaboration resulted in more readable view of data that is often repetitive and multitudinous. Through out development, I advocated for our end-users and sought to involve them at appropriate times to test for usability. This sustained communication with users helped me and my team identify new users as the project moved forward.
